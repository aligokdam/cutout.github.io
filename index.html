Cutout Effect Studio

Turn any photo into bold, flat, poster-style art.

This tool takes a photo and reduces it to a small number of flat color tones with clean, simplified edges — the classic "cutout" or posterized look, similar to a paper collage or screen print.

Everything runs locally in your browser. Your photo is never uploaded to a server.

How to use it

  1. Upload a photo — click the upload area, drag & drop, or press Enter.
  2. Adjust Number of Levels, Edge Simplicity and Edge Fidelity to shape the effect.
  3. Use Reset to Original or Reset All Settings any time to start over.
  4. Pick PNG or JPG and download your result.
